FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a CRB report?

A CRB (Credit Reference Bureau) report is a detailed record of your credit history in Kenya. It includes information about your loans, credit cards, mobile loans (like M-Shwari, Tala, Branch), and payment behavior. Lenders, employers, and landlords may check your CRB status before making decisions.

How long does it take to get my report?

Your CRB report is generated instantly after successful M-Pesa payment. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.

What's the difference between CRB Check and Clearance?

CRB Check (KES 50) gives you a detailed view of your credit report, including your credit score, loan history, and any negative listings.

CRB Clearance (KES 100) provides an official clearance certificate that you can present to employers, landlords, or lenders. It includes a verification code and is valid for 90 days.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We use encrypted connections and secure payment processing through M-Pesa via PayHero. Your personal information is never shared with third parties and is only used to generate your CRB report.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa payments only. When you click pay, an STK push notification will be sent directly to your phone — just enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the payment. No manual paybill entry needed!
